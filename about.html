The Practice

Use them as inspired.

Grandfather of plant essences, Dr. Edward Bach, suggested 4 drops, 4 times per day — ideally upon waking and sleeping, the pivot points of the day.

i

Drops

A few drops directly in the mouth or under the tongue — or into water, tea, or smoothies.

ii

Body points

Place drops on acupuncture points or chakras. Use before meditation or prayer.

iii

Space

Dilute and spray or diffuse through a room or home to shift the field you live in.

iv

The garden

Add drops to plant water and share with houseplants, growing flowers, trees, orchards, land.

The essence drops are seeds. If the soil is fertile, the essence may root; if the receiver isn’t receptive, it simply floats away.
